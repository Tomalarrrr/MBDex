About Results Driven

Results driven people, established in 2014 by Phil Southern, is committed to assisting busy Managing Directors free up time and improve decision making through tighter financial control and reporting.   If you believe your finance operation may be strengthened (or, worse, is holding you back) and you would benefit from some 'big business' thinking, contact the professionals at Results Driven now.



Phil Southern

An inquisitive and approachable finance professional, adaptable to a changing environment and able to challenge while remaining committed to the success of the team. Over 30 years’ experience in manufacturing, distribution, wholesale, retail and service operations with a focus for providing relevant financial information and direction.
